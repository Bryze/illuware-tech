About

IlluWare Technologies was founded by 3 innovative minds sharing a common goal in life, that is, “To make life as innovative as possible”. We have already worked on 2 start-ups in past. illuWare Technologies is our third attempt. At illuWare Technologies, we are working on a “Smart Torch”. What makes it “Smart” is that it makes use of internet to add values to the lives of our customers.

We believe that the “Torches” were created not only for providing illumination, but also for security and protection. Unfortunately, currently available torches fail in delivering the extras yet most important aspects of a torch. “Smart Torch” on the other hand is not like the rest. It makes use of GPS to send alerts to the loved ones of the user in case the user gets in some kind of trouble. Thus, ensuring a sense of safety and security to the user.
